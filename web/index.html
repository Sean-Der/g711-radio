G.711 Radio

This page loads the configured stream groups from the Go server. Expand a group, then open each feed in its own tab so the player only runs on a dedicated stream page.

Connection
Idle.

Group names, stream names, and ports come from config.local.json. Every stream is hard-coded for PCMU with a 12-byte header in front of each 160-byte audio frame. Click a group to expand it, clicking Listen opens that feed in a new tab, and ?stream=... links stop on a page with a Start Stream button instead of autoplaying.
